About

I am a postdoctoral researcher at the Machine Learning Genoa Center (MaLGa), where I work under the supervision of Lorenzo Rosasco.

Prior to that, I completed a PhD in applied mathematics in December 2023 in Toulouse, France, under the supervision of Francois Malgouyres and Francois Bachoc.

Research interests:

  • Physics-informed machine learning
  • Deep learning theory
  • Geometry of ReLU networks
  • Kernel methods
